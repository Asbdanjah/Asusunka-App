Privacy Policy Android

📅 Updated: June 28, 2026
🔐 VTU App – your data matters. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Virtual Top-Up (VTU) services on Android.

1. Information we collect

To provide reliable VTU services (airtime, data, bill payments), we may collect:

  • Account data – phone number, email, name (for registration & transaction history).
  • Transaction details – amount, service type, recipient info, and payment references.
  • Device & usage – Android version, device model, app version, and interaction logs (to improve performance).
  • Optional permissions – SMS (for auto‑reading OTP / transaction alerts) and camera (for scanning QR codes / documents) – only with your explicit consent.

We do not collect sensitive data like contacts, call logs, or location unless required for a specific feature (and with your permission).

2. How we use your data

  • ✅ Process VTU transactions (airtime, data bundles, bill payments).
  • ✅ Send transaction confirmations and service updates.
  • ✅ Improve app stability, security, and user experience.
  • ✅ Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • ✅ Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse.
🔒 encrypted storage 📊 analytics (anonymized) 📨 transactional emails/SMS

3. Data sharing & third parties

We never sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Payment gateways & mobile network operators – to complete transactions.
  • Service providers – cloud hosting, analytics (e.g., Firebase), and customer support tools.
  • Legal authorities – if required by law or to protect our rights.

📌 All third parties are contractually bound to protect your data.

4. Your choices & control

  • 🔹 Access & correction – update your profile and transaction history via the app.
  • 🔹 Permissions – manage SMS, camera, and storage permissions in your Android settings.
  • 🔹 Deletion – request account deletion by contacting support (data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law).
  • 🔹 Opt-out – you may disable non‑essential analytics in the app settings.

5. Data security

We implement industry‑standard safeguards:

  • 🔒 Encryption – all data in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES‑256).
  • 🔑 Access controls – restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • 🛡️ Regular audits – vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.

In case of a data breach, we will notify affected users and authorities as required by law.

6. Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via in‑app notification or email. The “Updated” date at the top of this page indicates the latest revision. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
